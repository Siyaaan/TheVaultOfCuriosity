**Being a jack of all trades can help you in interacting socially**       

	While some people despise being a jack of all trades, I think there's this benefit socially since you are able to relate and connect with other people's specialty faster.
	
	Try to explore, engage, and be adventurous in life and do not try to be like very jack of all trades so hard. Enjoy the process of discovery and with that, you also tend to enjoy meeting new people.
