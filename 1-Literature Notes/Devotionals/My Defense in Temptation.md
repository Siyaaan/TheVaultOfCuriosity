*"Thy word have I hid in mine heart, that I might not sin against thee".* Psalm 119:11

This chapter highlight the importance of having our faith and knowledge on the Word of God. It is our medium of communication with God. As we talk with Him, we may feel the gentle touch of His mercy and the presence of the Holy Spirit. So that if ever temptation tried to challenge us, we have the knowledge that we are not alone, that there is a much powerful presence working with us. The Bible is like our anchor which holds us still and stay grounded to our biblical values if we ever felt being swayed by temptation.  

The current situation in our environment is very vulnerable with temptation. It is not only because of how a single temptation can significantly influence us, but also because of some inconsequential influence which we thought of as just an unimportant and a nuisance on us. However, those small temptations are what makes us yield to it as it is always with us on our daily lives and it lives inside our mind continuously. It slowly engulf us until we give up, as this is not a battle of strength but of endurance.

One of our resolution for this is we have to also find other people who shares the same values as us and is guided by the principles of the bible. In such a way, we are able to form a stronger bond and connection with God's Work and Word.
