*"Thy word is a lamp unto my feet, and a light unto my path." Psalm 119:105*

Ellen G. White emphasizes that the Word of God is our guide for our actions, thinking, and purpose. The Bible is the standard of where we should base our principle. It is the gift from God for us to have a direction in our life so that we can meet him in the end and have everlasting life. Without a guide, we let ourselves be vulnerable on the wickedness of the world which leads us part ways to where the truth is.

The very concerning things when looking at our world today is that not everyone of us has a lamp that would guide everyone of us to Christ. Some of us neglect the word on purpose and some our not enlightened. Therefore, it is our duty, as the bearer of the lamp of Christ to give light on others, fill their lamp fire, and fulfil our duty to show others the way to salvation.

We may not be able to help and save everyone, which is a disappointing truth. However, as long as we try our best to hold our lamp and keep the light in us, we might inspire others with our resolve to continue to where Christ is.
