 **If you are feeling lost, evaluating where your current self is the best way to start **
 
	By starting where your current self is, you avoid the less important things and focus on the things that can keep you on track with your goals. For example here in my Odyssey of Curiosity vault, knowing the important things which can help me in the long run is much beneficial than just following what sparked my interest. Yes curiosity is a thing, but myself should start first on where I can benefit and have interest at the same time. Stabilize your schedule first on how you handle this. And this should not feel like a burden or a duty since you know on yourself that all of inside this is in your interest. Do you think you can enjoy a book in the end if  you realize that there's not much things you can benefit from? That's is why it is important to know that your interest aligns with your goal.

Evaluating the why on every thing you start can help you overcome any how. Or in other words, analyze carefully if what I will be doing (reading, essay, starting hobby) can actually help me benefit with my goals in the long run.

Anchor it in yourself!
