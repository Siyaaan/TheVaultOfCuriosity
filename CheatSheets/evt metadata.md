| **Part of the Wrapper** | **What it tells you** | **Why it's useful**                          |
| ----------------------- | --------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **`.value()`**          | "The Content"         | What the user typed.                         |
| **`.code()`**           | "The Key"             | Did they hit Enter? Escape? Space?           |
| **`.modifiers()`**      | "The State"           | Were they holding `Ctrl`, `Alt`, or `Shift`? |
| **`.timestamp()`**      | "The Time"            | How fast were they typing?                   |
