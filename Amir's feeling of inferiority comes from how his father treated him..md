Amir's feeling of inferiority comes from how his father treated him. Baba saw him as a failure who couldn't inherit his passions. Amir knew this. Even though he was hurt by it, he eventually found what his passionate for, which is reading books and making story. We can clearly see that he inherited his mother. Amir then created his first story and shows it to Baba, Rahim, and Hassan. Of course, Baba complimented him though he was not proud for it. Rahim then recognizes Amir's talent and motivates him to continue it as he has greater potential than the majority of the writers. He then shows it to Hassan and is proudly of Amir as well. However, Hassan questioned his first story which captured a plot hole. That was the first time Amir discovered what  plot-hole is. He feels like what he is the only thing he was passionate about, yet he feel inferior as he was criticized and questioned by someone who was illiterate. How ironic indeed. He tried to answer Hassan's question, but words couldn't come out of his mouth.
