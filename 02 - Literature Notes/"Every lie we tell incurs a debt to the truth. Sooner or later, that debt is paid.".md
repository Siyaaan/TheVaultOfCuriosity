Lying is a coping mechanism that people use to hide their insecurity. And in Chernobyl, people lie because they have this strong image of something that they are trying to protect. They are trying to protect themselves, their legacy, their pride, and the USSR. However, lying provides a weak foundation, because it is not even real in the first place, and that keeps on stacking and adding the weight every time a lie is told. And when that foundation is not able to handle the weight of the lies, everything is destroyed, and everyone is affected. This is the example of how Chernobyl not only affected itself but the people and its country. Even people who are not involved is included because of how catastrophic lying is. That is why people should be reminded of the danger lying provides.
